SPI (Spatial Intelligence) Research
( / )

SPI Research is a non-profit research group within Pseudo Lab where we review papers, conduct research, and construct projects to tackle Spatial AI for real-world applications. We are a non-profit community driving the popularization of machine learning research, with the ultimate goal of bringing together people interested in Spatial AI to collaboratively create knowledge and contribute to solving real-world challenges.
